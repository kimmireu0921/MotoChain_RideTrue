The Problem

Two markets broken by the same root cause

No trustless, tamper-proof record of what actually happened — to the bike, or to the rider.

🏍️

Secondhand Motorcycle Market

Odometers are tampered, accident records concealed, and titles manipulated. Buyers on Naver Cafe or Bungae Jangter have no way to verify a seller's claims — costing money and creating real safety risks.

📋

Motorcycle Insurance in Korea

Premiums are set by age and registration year — blunt proxies with no connection to actual behavior. Safe young riders who leave a family plan face premiums that nearly double overnight, despite zero change in real risk.

Incentive Design

Mechanic Incentive Loop

The registry is only as trustworthy as the mechanics who write to it. The incentive model must make fraud unprofitable — not just penalized when caught.

🏆

Tiered Reputation Staking

Mechanics begin with a small stake and unlock higher write-access tiers — and higher fees — as reputation grows. A top-tier mechanic has far more to lose. Fraud becomes exponentially more costly as success increases.

⏳

60-Day Escrow Window

Fees are held in escrow after each submission. If a dispute or inconsistency is flagged within this window, the fee is clawed back along with a stake penalty. The reward is only real if the record holds.

🔍

Cross-Validator Challenges

Any verified mechanic or inspector can challenge a submitted record within the escrow window. A successful challenge earns a share of the slashed stake — community policing with no central authority.

✅ Honest Mechanic

Fee released after 60 days. Reputation score increases. Unlocks higher tier, higher fees per entry, and a growing protocol credential visible to all customers.

❌ Fraudulent Entry

Fee clawed back. Stake slashed — the higher the tier, the larger the slash. Tier downgraded. Record flagged on-chain permanently. Challenger earns a reward.

Why staking isn't a burden: Mechanics already pay large upfront fees to brands like Honda to access legitimacy and customers. Protocol staking replaces that cost with a credential that is publicly auditable, portable across platforms, and earns back returns through honest participation. Honda fees are sunk costs. Protocol stakes grow in value with reputation.
